# Failover Drill Postmortem

This report logs the multi-region failover drill conducted on November 12, 2025.

## Test Scope
The objective was to simulate a complete outage of the US-East-1 AWS region and verify the automatic and manual failover procedures to US-West-2.

## Key Metrics
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective)**: 12 minutes (Target: < 15 minutes)
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective)**: 4.2 seconds (Target: < 10 seconds)
- **Database Lag**: Active-Active database replication was within 1.5 seconds prior to simulated isolation.

## Incident Log & Manual Steps
During the failover, automatic Route53 routing policies failed to update active health check files.
- **Manual Intervention Required**: SRE Priya Nair had to manually log in to the AWS console to trigger the primary DNS record cutover.
- **Cache Quirks**: Standard client-side DNS cache TTL of 60 seconds delayed the propagation by an additional 120 seconds in older browsers.
- **Mitigation**: Update all CloudFront DNS configurations to strictly enforce a 30-second maximum TTL parameter.
